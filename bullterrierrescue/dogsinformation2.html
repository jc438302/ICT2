Name: Zeus

If you’ve known a bull Terrier before, you’re probably aware that we tend to think that we’re actually humans. Don’t be fooled by the rough exterior I’m a person, I promise…just a redheaded gentleman looking for love. Sweet, gentle and kind-natured, I adore children and would be a lovely family addition. I do not want to share my family with other canine pets. I haven’t been around cats so I can’t promise that I’d tolerate their presence, so caution is advised. 6ft fences will be sufficient to keep me safely contained. I’ve been kept mainly outside at my foster home, but I’d love to be inside. That’s where the people are…and as I’ve mentioned, I am one of you, remember? I even love human food, so take care not to leave it where it might tempt me! I have basic obedience but I’m a clever boy and would thrive with your time and guidance. At least 30 minutes’ walk daily will see a goofy smile plastered to my face. I live for your love, attention and to feel the wind in my face. If you’re looking for an affectionate gentleman, please submit an adoption application my Adoption Fee: $350 (includes desexing, C5 vaccination, yearly heartworm injection, parasite treatment, microchipping & transfer of microchip into new owner’s name)
